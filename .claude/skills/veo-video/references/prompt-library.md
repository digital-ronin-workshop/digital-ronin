### Timelapse Template
```
A timelapse of [BUILDER/ACTOR] [ACTION] in/at [LOCATION],
[MATERIALS/TOOLS]. The [ENVIRONMENT CHANGE] as they [PREP ACTION].
A [STRUCTURE/RESULT] takes shape. [BUILD SEQUENCE], [FEATURE DEVELOPMENT].
As shadows shift, [TRANSFORMATION from START STATE to END STATE].
[FINAL TOUCHES]. The [ACTOR] steps back, [EMOTION], admiring the
[COMPLETED RESULT] nestled among [SURROUNDINGS].
```

### First/Last Frame Timelapse
```
Every change should appear as a physical build-up, morph, or
time-lapse assembly, never as a dissolve, opacity blend, or crossfade.
Maintain consistent camera alignment and parallax throughout.
Static tripod, locked-off camera, no camera movement.
```
